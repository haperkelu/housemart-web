关于好识买

好识买致力于提供真实、透明、全面的房产信息，包括出售出租盘源，价格，历史记录等。透过我们的移动客户端（app），包括苹果手机和安卓手机，有兴趣的买方或承租方可以轻松便捷地浏览房产信息、与经纪人在线聊天，同时保护自己的隐私，不用担心骚扰电话。

好识买的终极目标在创造买方（承租方）和卖方（出租方）的交易平台。有鉴于目前中国房产市场信息不对称、虚假信息充斥的现象，好识买透过自主研发的技术，提供线上的聊天空间，让有兴趣的买方、卖方、承租方、出租方、经纪人轻松地在线问询；并向全社会免费提供一目了然、全方位的流通盘数据。好识买的用户新体验：免费、轻松、便捷、无骚扰。

好识买于2013年成立，目前在上海提供房产服务。

About HouseMart

HouseMart specializes in offering genuine and real-time residential property listing, pricing and other information to the general public. We also serve as an exchange platform on which buyers and sellers connect and communicate in respect of listed residential properties, whether for sale or for rent. Through our APP, downloaded either through Apple’s iOS App Store or through other Android smartphone devices, a prospective buyer or renter can browse residential properties of interest, save the property of interest, or send free messages to the person listing that property (whether to make inquiries or book an appointment for viewing).

Our goal is to change the way residential property transactions are done in China, where false information and market inefficiencies are prevalent. To reduce information asymmetry, we strive to create an online environment where buyers, sellers, landlords, renters as well as property agents can have easy access to free, up-to-date and genuine information on the residential property market. To improve efficiency, our proprietary technologies allow users to communicate and exchange information for free.

HouseMart is founded in 2013 and currently offers its services only in Shanghai.
